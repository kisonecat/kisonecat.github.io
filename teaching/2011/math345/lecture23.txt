prime divisors
Each natural number greater than or equal to 2 either is a product of prime numbers or is itself a prime number. Cor: Each integer, except 1 and 1, is divisible by some prime number. Let p be a prime number. Then for all integers x and y, if p divides xy, then p divides x or p divides y.
