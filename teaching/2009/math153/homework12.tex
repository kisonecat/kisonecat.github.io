\item[(b)] Divide the Taylor series for $(1 - \cos x)$ by $x^2$ to find

$$
\frac{1 - \cos x}{x^2}
$$

l'H\^opital's rule.

  $x^3$) of a Taylor series for $f(x)$ around $0$.  Using this Taylor
  series, determine $f^{(3)}(0)$ without performing any
  differentiation.
